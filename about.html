Eugyoung (Eugy) Han

I am a 2nd year PhD student in Communication at Stanford University. I earned my B.S. in Cognitive Science from Brown University in 2020. My research interests are broadly about what cognitive processes (e.g., perception of self and social surroundings; making moral judgements) look like when people embody digital identities in virtual environments. I am curious about how these findings can help shape virtual cultures and laws. 

During my free time, I enjoy writing short fiction and lounging in libraries. I also enjoy reading about the future and the many, interesting paths that technology can lead us to.
