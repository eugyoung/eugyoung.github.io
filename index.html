About Me

Hi! I'm Eugy, a 3rd year PhD candidate in Communication at Stanford University. I'm currently advised by Professor Jeremy Bailenson at the Virtual Human Interaction Lab (VHIL). I earned my B.S. in Cognitive Science from Brown University in 2020. During my undergrad, I was advised by Professor Bill Warren at the Virtual Environment Navigation Lab (VENLab).

I'm interested in studying what cognitive processes look like when people embody avatars in social virtual environments. Ultimately, I'm curious about how these findings can help shape virtual cultures and their laws.

My current projects include conducting one of the largest + longest studies on social interactions in the metaverse. During my free time, I enjoy writing sci-fi short stories and exploring new coffee shops. Feel free to reach out if you want to chat!
